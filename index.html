LETTER FROM THE SECRETARY-GENERAL

Distinguished Delegates, Observers and Esteemed Guests,

On behalf of the Secretariat, it is my distinct honour to extend to you all a welcome to first session of ATO Model United Nations Conference.

We are living in a time of inovation. With every ticking of the clock, the world is heading towards to advancement and development at a rapid pace, be it in technological progress, political reforms and medical discovery. Ceaseless changes of the planet as a result of globalization, rapid development of technology and the armament race between countries, lead to many inevitable conclusions. Conclusions are seemingly considered as death, diseases for instance COVID-19 and war on every corner. Nevertheless it is through these times of conflict that solidarity and cooperation come forward.

Therefore we, the global citizens, we should beware of theoretical illusions by going after the attainment of experience, culling knowledge from all available resources in the age of information and planning our common future with distinctive intentness.

As we pursue the ideals of the new world, we could not be expected to set history aside in determining the issues to be discussed by the next generation of leaders.ATOMUN strives to create a collaborative and professional platform for young people to grow personally and intellectually. In the first session of ATOMUN we are eager to introduce our United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees Committee which will be taking place in today and focusing on the lives of refugees who were victims of the Russia-Ukraine war. And our second committee World Health Organization will be taking place in today and debating on human life after COVİD-19. And in one of our committee we will be discussing impacts of global warming. And lastly The Hunger Games committe. Our committees will introduce delegates a range of global challenges, and to provide culturally diverse perspectives on a variety of issues.

We are conference for delegates of all skill levels and MUN experience, providing opportunities for all students to improve their knowledge, develop their critical thinking skills, and improve their public speaking abilities and confidence.

As the ATOMUN family, we are excited to embark on this innovative and unique journeywith you and rewarding MUN experience that would go a long way in moulding the attendees into the future leaders. We look forward to see you at ATO Model United Nations Conference in 20-21 April, 2022. I hope it will be the greatest start for ATOMUN.

Sincerely,

Asya Gül Battal

Secretary-General of ATOMUN 2022
